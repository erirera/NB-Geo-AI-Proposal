🚀 Exciting new research proposal: **NB Geo-AI - Automated Geological Contact Mapping!** 🌍

I've put together an interactive dashboard mockup to visualize a proposed deep-learning approach for geological mapping in New Brunswick. The goal? To train a U-Net segmentation model on open-access NRCan airborne geophysical data (magnetics, radiometrics, gravity) to automatically predict lithological contacts! 

This proposed workflow aims to rapidly identify "Revision Priority Zones"—areas where AI predictions strongly disagree with existing bedrock geology maps, helping prioritize expensive field-truthing campaigns. 

The dashboard (built with Leaflet & custom CSS) demonstrates what the expected output of this model will look like, featuring simulated predicted contacts and MC Dropout uncertainty heatmaps.

Check out the mockup and let me know your thoughts on applying deep learning to high-resolution geophysics!
